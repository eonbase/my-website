About

EON BASE ARCHITECTS integrates the disciplines of architecture, interior design, furniture, lighting, and product research and design. Current projects include residential, multifamily housing, commercial & industrial buildings, corporate offices, hospitality, & furniture.

The pursuit of absolute uniqueness and quality of our efforts implies that we follow a unique method of design development. We have a concrete team that is highly skilled and is able to transform the dream project of a client into reality. Their inspiration and involvement also ensures a positive outcome in every project. We also aim to create each design with an interpersonal and holistic yet sustainable and practical approach.
